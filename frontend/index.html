About SiliconCORE

Founded in 2015, SiliconCORE specializes in bringing high-quality refurbished laptops to customers who demand premium performance without the premium price tag. We source our devices from corporate lease returns, educational institutions, and quality trade-ins.

Each laptop in our inventory goes through our comprehensive 25-point refurbishment process to ensure it meets our strict quality standards. We replace worn components, thoroughly clean each device, and install fresh operating systems with all necessary updates.

Our mission is to make quality computing accessible while reducing electronic waste. Every refurbished laptop we sell keeps harmful e-waste out of landfills and puts money back in your pocket.
